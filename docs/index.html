Neon Wallet
The Neon Wallet is a cross-platform light wallet that allows you to interact with the Neo blockchain without syncing your computer.

Downloads

  • Windows: Neon.exe
  • Mac: Neon.dmg
  • Linux: Neon.appimage or Neon.deb
